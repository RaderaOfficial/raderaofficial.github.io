Radera delivers a mix from Rawstyle to Uptempo, with hit releases like “ON AND ON AND ON”, “TOGETHER”, and “BLACK WIDOW”.

With his extensive discography, he adapts easily to any lineup and set length. Whether you need Rawstyle, Uptempo, or a mix, Radera delivers a set that fits.

Radera is not just a DJ. He is an artist with a live experience like no other.
